About / Philosophy

At Gen X Shred, we recognize that the psychology of changing and the WHY you want to make the change is more important to successfully completing your goal than the details of HOW you’ll make the change.

We differ from other fitness programs in that we use motivational interviewing techniques to inspire you to overcome ambivalence and implement healthy habit changes.

Our Five Fundamental Principles

The HOW is generic—there are multiple ways to lose body fat or gain muscle. The secret sauce is finding your internal motivations and codifying habits until they’re second nature.

Sleep is Life

Sleep 7–9 hours every night and maintain consistent bed/wake times—even on weekends.

Calories In, Calories Out

Track your daily calories and eat in a 5–15 % deficit based on TDEE.

Protein is King

Consume 0.8–1.2 g protein per pound of body weight daily.

Adaptation Engine

Train with weights ≥4 × /week, progressively overload, and switch up your split every 4–6 weeks.

Lifestyle Guardrails

Manage stress; limit alcohol to 1–2 drinks/week; avoid ultra-processed foods.
